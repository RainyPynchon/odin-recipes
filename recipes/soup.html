Leek Soup

Showcasing the unrivalled leek soup.

An espeically delicious soup featuring leek and potatoes. Both fantastic winter crops taken even further in combination with heart and body warming cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of potatoes
  • 1 kg of leeks
  • 250 ml of cream
  • Thyme - preferably fresh
  • Sufficent stock to cover the vegetables

Steps

  1. Roughly chop leeks and potatoes
  2. Add to pan and stir until a bit of brown color appear
  3. Add stock until it covers the vegetables and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the potatoes are tender
  4. Add cream and a bit of thyme
  5. Taste and season with salt, pepper and nutmeg
